# Features

## Customer Ordering

1. Scan the QR code or open `/menu`.
2. Browse available products.
3. Add products to the cart and submit an order.
4. Receive an order confirmation.

## Vendor Operations

- Supabase-authenticated vendor login
- Vendor dashboard
- Order history
- Kitchen workflow: Pending → Preparing → Ready → Completed
- Inventory quantity and reorder-level controls
- Low-stock alerts

## Business Analytics

Completed orders provide:

- Today's revenue and completed-order count
- Overall revenue and profit
- Best-selling product
- Seven-day revenue chart
- Dashboard low-stock alerts

## Planned AI Business Advisor

The vendor will ask business questions such as:

- How was my business today?
- What should I prepare tomorrow?
- Which product is most profitable?

The planned backend-only provider is OpenRouter using `poolside/laguna-m.1:free`.
